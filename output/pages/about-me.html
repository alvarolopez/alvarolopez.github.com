About me

Activity

I work for the Spanish National Research Council at the IFCA's Advanced Computing and e-Science Group.

Cloud stuff

  • I am the maintainer of the VOMS authentication module for OpenStack and the VOMS authentication plugin for novaclient. I also developed the cASO accounting extractor, used to collect the OpenStack records in EGI's Cloud Infrastructure.
  • I am an ATC for OpenStack. I have been an ATC for other projects as well.
  • I help to maintain the OCCI interface for OpenStack. You can find the code here and in my own repo.

Useful things

  • Script for generating GGUS reports for NGI_IBERGRID, that can be used for other NGIs as well.
  • For the EGI presentations I use this beamer theme.
